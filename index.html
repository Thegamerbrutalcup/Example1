Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor,
Crafted with Love

Born in a small kitchen in South Mumbai, Saffron & Spice has grown from a cherished family recipe book into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations. Every dish carries generations of knowledge — slow-cooked gravies, hand-ground masalas, and techniques passed down through kitchens we were never meant to leave.

We believe fine dining doesn't mean forgetting your roots. It means honoring them — beautifully.
